# VSANS Instrument Publications

If you have written a paper that reports measurements using this instrument and you do not see it in the list below, or if the paper is listed but the information is incorrect or out of date, please inform [contact details omitted].

## Citation Requirements

If you write a paper or give a presentation that reports measurements using the vSANS Instrument, please ensure the following:

*   **Acknowledgements:** Include an acknowledgement to the NSF, and to NIST if there are no NIST coauthors.
*   **Instrument Reference:** Include a reference to the paper describing the vSANS Instrument: 
    *   "The very small angle neutron scattering instrument at the National Institute of Standards and Technology", Barker J, et al. *J Appl. Cryst.* 55 (2) 271 (2022).
*   **Data Reduction Reference:** If you reduced and/or analyzed your data using the Igor Macros, include the reference:
    *   "Reduction and Analysis of SANS and USANS Data using Igor Pro", Kline, S. R. *J Appl. Cryst.* 39 (6), 895 (2006).
*   **Analysis Software:** If you analyzed your data using the SasView application, please follow the guidance outlined on the SasView publications site.

## Editorial Review
All papers with NIST coauthors must be reviewed and approved by the Washington Editorial Review Board (WERB). WERB reviews focus on:
1. Measurement uncertainties
2. Use of SI units
3. Brand names
