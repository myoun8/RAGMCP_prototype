# BT-7 Double Focusing Triple-Axis Spectrometer

As part of the modernization of the thermal neutron spectrometers, a state-of-the-art thermal neutron instrument has been installed at the BT-7 thermal beam port.

## Technical Specifications and Features

### Monochromators and Beam Control
*   **Monochromators:** Choice of 20 cm by 20 cm double-focusing monochromators:
    *   PG(002)
    *   PG(004) (available over select energies)
    *   Cu(220)
*   **Beam Conditioning:** 
    *   A velocity selector is installed in the reactor beam to suppress unwanted neutrons.
    *   A PG filter is available.
    *   Choice of collimations in the reactor beam.

### Sample Stage
*   Incorporates an independent magnet axis.
*   Includes standard motorized sample orientation and rotation angles.

### Analyzer and Detector System
The analyzer and detector system is housed in a single unit on air pads and is designed to be self-contained for easy interchangeability.
*   **Capabilities:**
    *   Horizontal focusing capability.
    *   Crystal array coupled with a position-sensitive detector for simultaneous collection of data over a range of $(Q, E)$.
    *   Choice of various conventional collimators.
*   **Control:** All settings are remotely managed under computer control.

### Polarized Beam Operation
Guide fields are incorporated throughout the instrument to enable polarized beam operation utilizing optically-pumped $\text{He}^3$ polarizers.
