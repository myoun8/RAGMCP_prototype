# BT-7 Triple-Axis Summer School Experiment

This page provides information and resources related to the Triple-Axis Summer School experiments and tutorials conducted at the BT-7 spectrometer.

## Recent Schools and Experiments

### 29th CHRNS School on Methods and Applications of Neutron Spectroscopy
*   **Dates:** July 17th to July 21st, 2023.

### 27th CHRNS School on Methods and Applications of Neutron Spectroscopy
*   **Dates:** January 28 - February 11, 2022 (Virtual Only).

### 2019 Dynamics Summer School
*   **Dates:** June 22 through June 26, 2019.
*   **Experimental Focus:** Colossal Magnetoresistive Spin Dynamics.
*   **Analysis Software:** SpinW program is used to analyze spin wave data.
*   **Resources:** [BT-7 examples](https://www.nist.gov/ncnr/bt-7-triple-axis-summer-school-experiment) provide types of data obtainable on BT-7 with brief explanations.
